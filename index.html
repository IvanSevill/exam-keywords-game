Welcome to Active Recall Game!

This app helps you memorize your exam answers by forcing you to recall the keywords.

Game Modes:
  • Normal: Type the full answer. The app will detect keywords as you type. You can't proceed until you find them all.
  • Learning: Like Normal, but gives you a hint (reveals a letter) every 3 seconds to help you when stuck.
  • Fast (Hard): No text area. Just type the exact keywords in individual boxes.
  • Study Explorer: Browse all questions, answers and keywords before playing. Great for reviewing.
